    Welcome to Jacob's House¶

    A Second Chance, A New Beginning¶

    Jacob’s House is a structured sober living program designed to help individuals rebuild their lives after addiction. We offer a tiered housing model that meets residents where they are in their recovery journey and guides them toward independence.

    Why Choose Jacob’s House?¶

    • Structure and Accountability: A clear path to recovery and reintegration.
    • Empathy-Driven Support: Founded by someone with lived experience in recovery.
    • Community and Growth: A safe place to build trust, skills, and a brighter future.

    Our Programs¶

    Transition Housing¶

    A highly structured environment focusing on recovery and job readiness. Residents participate in mandatory meetings and follow strict rules for stability.

    Sober Living¶

    Moderated structure with curfews, chores, and peer accountability. Ideal for those transitioning to greater independence.

    Advanced Living¶

    Minimal rules with peer voting and self-management for residents demonstrating sustained progress.

    Milestones Achieved¶

    • 45+ lives changed: Over 4 years, Jacob’s House has helped individuals rebuild with long-term sobriety and stability.
    • Safe, structured environment: Ensuring growth and transformation for all residents.
    • Expanding our mission: Future plans include housing for women and children.

    Ready to Begin Your Journey?¶

    Jacob’s House is here to guide you every step of the way. Whether you're starting fresh or looking for a supportive community, we offer a safe place to grow and thrive.
